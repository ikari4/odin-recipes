Apple Pie

Home

Description

Donna's famous apple pie is a delicious dessert. It uses a variety of apple types to create a complex flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Flour
  • 1/2 Tsp Nutmeg
  • 1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
  • Dash Salt
  • 6 Cups Apples, Thinly Sliced
  • 2 Tbsp Butter

Directions

  1. Heat Over to 425F
  2. Prepare Pastry
  3. Stir together sugar, flour, nutmeg, cinnamon and Salt
  4. Mix with apples
  5. Turn into pastry-lined pie pan
  6. Dot with Butter
  7. Cover with top crust which has slits in it
  8. Seal and flute
  9. Cover edge with 2-3" strig of aluminum foil last 15 min of baking
  10. Bake 45-50 min or until crust is brown and juice begins to bubble through slits in crust
